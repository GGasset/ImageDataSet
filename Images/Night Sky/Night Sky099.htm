DescriptionNight sky.jpg

The area surrounding Coudersport is known for some of the darkest skies in the east. Erie is the closest major city, 2 hours away.

There was a full moon this night or even more stars would be seen.

Got 15 minutes of 30" exposures until condensation started to form.
